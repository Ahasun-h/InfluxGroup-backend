## 📱 **Responsive Design**

### **Mobile Adaptations:**
- **Timeline:** Vertical layout (alternating removed on mobile)
- **Grids:** Collapse to 1-2 columns
- **Typography:** Scales down (text-[10px] → text-xs → text-sm)
- **Padding:** Reduced on smaller screens
- **Buttons:** Stack vertically on mobile
- **Cards:** Full-width on mobile

### **Breakpoints:**
- **< 768px:** Mobile (single column, stacked)
- **768px - 1023px:** Tablet (2 columns)
- **1024px+:** Desktop (3-4 columns, full layout)

---

## 🚀 **Performance Considerations**

### **Optimizations:**
- ✅ **Lazy Loading:** v-motion-slide-visible directives
- ✅ **Staggered Animations:** :delay="index * 100"
- ✅ **Computed Properties:** Reactive filtering
- ✅ **Component Icons:** Tree-shakeable imports
- ✅ **Responsive Images:** Object-cover for performance

### **Animation Strategy:**
- **Initial Load:** Hero section animations
- **Scroll Triggered:** Each section animates in when visible
- **Hover Effects:** Scale, color, shadow transitions
- **Stagger Delays:** 100ms increments for visual flow
